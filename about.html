About Us

We are a family-owned business that has been producing and selling high-quality coconut products since 2020. We are based in Kurunegala, Sri Lanka, where we have our own coconut plantation and processing facility. We are passionate about coconuts and their amazing benefits for health, beauty, and environment.

Our mission is to provide our customers with the best coconut products that are organic, ethically sourced, and eco-friendly. Our vision is to become a leading brand in the global market for coconut products and to support the local farmers and communities who work with us.

We are proud to offer a wide range of products such as coconut oil, coconut milk, coconut milk powder, coconut butter, coconut water, and more. All our products are made from fresh and mature coconuts, harvested and processed with care and quality standards. We use natural and sustainable methods, without any chemicals or additives.

Tropical Elixar, a distinguished coconut company, stands as a beacon of excellence in the realm of tropical delights. Specializing in the production of premium coconut-based products, this innovative enterprise has carved a niche for itself with a diverse range of offerings that redefine the essence of coconut indulgence.

At the heart of Tropical Elixar's portfolio is its flagship product – coconut oil. Meticulously extracted from the finest coconuts, the oil embodies purity and richness. Whether used for culinary masterpieces or as a beauty elixir, Tropical Elixar's coconut oil is a testament to the company's commitment to quality.

In addition to the liquid gold of coconut oil, Tropical Elixar boasts an array of refreshing beverages. The company's coconut water captures the essence of tropical paradise in every sip, offering a hydrating and rejuvenating experience. Perfectly balancing sweetness and freshness, it's a natural source of vitality.

For those seeking convenience without compromising on flavor, Tropical Elixar presents coconut milk powder. Crafted with precision, this powdered delight retains the authentic taste of coconut milk, ready to elevate culinary creations with a tropical twist. From curries to desserts, it's a versatile ingredient that adds a touch of the exotic to any dish.

To cater to the discerning palate, Tropical Elixar introduces coconut butter – a velvety spread that encapsulates the essence of coconut in a rich, creamy texture. Ideal for both sweet and savory applications, it's a luscious addition to breakfast spreads or gourmet recipes.

Beyond their exceptional products, Tropical Elixar is committed to sustainability, sourcing coconuts responsibly to support local communities and ecosystems. The company's dedication to quality, innovation, and ethical practices solidifies its position as a leader in the coconut industry, bringing the tropical allure of coconut into homes around the world. Indulge in the richness of Tropical Elixar's offerings – where every product is a sip or a spoonful of tropical paradise.
