Frequently Asked Questions

  • Our coconut products are versatile and can be used for cooking, baking, beauty, health, and wellness. Some of our products, such as coconut oil, coconut milk, and coconut butter can be used as substitutes for dairy products in vegan and lactose-free diets. Our products are also gluten-free, non-GMO, and organic certified.

  • Our coconut products have a long shelf life and can be stored at room temperature or in the refrigerator, depending on the product. For coconut oil, you can store it in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. For coconut milk and butter, you can store them in the refrigerator after opening and use them within a few days. For coconut water, you can store it in the refrigerator and drink it within 24 hours. For coconut milk powder, you can store it in an airtight container in a cool, dry place or in the freezer.
